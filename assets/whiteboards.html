Editing whiteboards

Note: you can't create a new whiteboard from your phone yet. To do that, visit this link.

From the main screen, click on the "Whiteboards" button. You might have to wait a bit for the next screen to show up. When it does, you will see a list of the whiteboards that your household has created. Select one of them to edit it.

When editing a whiteboard, you will see text at the top indicating when the whiteboard was last edited. For example, if no-one has written on the whiteboard for 6 hours, it will say "last updated 6 hours ago".

After you've written on the whiteboard, touch "Save" to save the whiteboard. A notification will pop up telling you whether the whiteboard was successfully saved. If it wasn't, try hitting save again. Sometimes it just needs a nudge.
